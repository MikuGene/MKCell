MKCell Analysis Landscape


 For Bulk RNA-seq:

   -- Cell composition --

** MKCell allows to estimate the cell composition when you upload bulk RNA-seq data. **

   -- State estimation --

** MKCell allows to estimate the samples state when you upload bulk RNA-seq data. **

   -- Virus Alignment --

** MKCell allows to estimate the virus infection when you upload bulk RNA-seq data. **


 For ScRNA-seq:

   -- Cell recognition --

** MKCell allows to identify known cell phenotype when you upload single cell RNA-seq data. **

   -- Cell state --

** MKCell allows to estimate each cell state when you upload single cell RNA-seq data. **


 For Custom Analysis:

   -- Correlation Test --

** MKCell allows to calculate the correlation between the two gene groups from each gene. **

   -- Difference Test --

** MKCell allows to identify differentially expressed genes between two gene expression profiles. **

   -- Matrix Process --

** MKCell allows a variety of commonly used processing of the matrix. **

   -- HeatMap Plot --

** MKCell allows to plot a heatmap of a gene expression profile. **

   -- Clustering --

** MKCell allows to unsupervised clustering of samples in your gene expression profiles. **


** All the analysis will be performed on the terminal server. Close the web-page will not affect the analysis process after you upload data. You can check your recent results on each history button. **
